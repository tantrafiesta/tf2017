ABOUT

Indian Institute of Information technology, Nagpur & Lemures Technologies proudly presents 2 Days bootcamp on Cisco Networking and Cyber Security at IIIT, Nagpur

WHAT WHEN WHERE

  • WHAT:Workshop on Cisco Networking and Cyber Security
  • WHEN: Saturday, Sunday 7 - 8 October 2017
  • TIME: 9:30am - 6:00pm
  • WHERE: Seminar Hall, Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur.

BENIFITS

  • National Level Certification from IIIT Nagpur & Lemures Technologies
  • 75% Practical & 25% Theory
