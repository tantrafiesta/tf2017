About IIIT Nagpur?

INDIAN INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IIITN), is one of the Indian Institutes of Technology in the country, set up with the objective of making available favilities for higher education, research and training in various feilds of Information Technology. The institute is committed to get itself recognized as a leader in Information Technology related science and engineering.IIIT Nagpur is to create an ambiance in which new ideas, research and scholarship flourish and from which leaders and innovators and IT-entrepreneurs of tomorrow emerge.

What is Tantra Fiesta

Tantra Fiesta is one of the Technical events held at Indian Institute of Information Technology Nagpur.Tantra Fiesta includes many technical events like Coding, Search War, Graphic Designing, Innovative Exhibition, RobotHunger Games and many more.
